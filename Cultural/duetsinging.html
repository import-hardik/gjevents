Rules and Regulations:

  • Duet pairs may choose any theme-related song of their preference.
  • Songs should be appropriate for a college audience and must not contain explicit or offensive language.
  • Each duet pair will be allocated a maximum of 4 minutes for their performance.
  • Accompaniment may be live or pre-recorded, and duet pairs are responsible for providing their own backing tracks or instruments.
  • Lip-syncing or miming is strictly prohibited.
  • Duet pairs must incorporate harmonization into their performance.
  • The venue will provide basic sound equipment including microphones and speakers.
  • The decision of the judges and organizers is final and binding.
  • Winners will receive special prizes.
  • All participants will receive certificates of participation.
  • If there are 5 or fewer entries, the event will be canceled. In that case, participants can choose either to receive a full refund or transfer to another event of the same price.
  • In case of an event clash with any other committee event, no refund will be applicable.

Judging Criteria:

  • Vocal Quality: Clarity, tone, pitch accuracy, harmony, range, and control of both members.
  • Stage Presence: Confidence, engagement with the audience, synchronization between members, and overall charisma.
  • Interpretation: Emotional expression, connection to the song's lyrics and melody, and artistic interpretation by both members.
  • Overall Performance: Creativity, originality, and entertainment value demonstrated by both members.
