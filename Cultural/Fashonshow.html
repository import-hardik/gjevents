Fashion Show

Rules and Regulations

Theme:

  • The theme of the fashion show is Cultural and Bollywood.
  • Participants must adhere to the theme in their outfit selection and presentation.

Outfit Selection:

  • Participants are responsible for selecting their outfits based on the theme.
  • Outfits should be appropriate for a fashion show setting and must not violate any decency standards.

Accessories and Styling:

  • Participants may accessorize their outfits as desired, including jewels, footwear, and hair accessories.
  • Hair-styling and makeup should complement the overall look and theme of the outfit.

Event Cancellations and Refund Policy:

  • If there are 5 or fewer entries, the event will be cancelled. In that case, participants can choose either to receive a full refund or transfer to another event of the same price.
  • In case of an event clash with any other committee event, no refund will be applicable.

Ramp Walk:

  • Participants will showcase their outfits by performing a ramp walk in front of the audience and judges.
  • Walks should exude confidence, poise, and grace, with attention to posture and body language.

Judging Criteria:

  • Fashion Sense: Creativity, style, and interpretation of the theme.
  • Presentation: Confidence, stage presence, and charisma during the ramp walk.
  • Coordination (for group category): Harmony and coherence of outfits within the group theme.
  • Overall Impact: Visual appeal, originality, and audience engagement.

Prizes and Recognition:

  • Winners will receive special prizes.
  • All participants will receive certificates of participation.
