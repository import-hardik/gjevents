Rules and Regulations

  • For individual performances, the timing should not exceed 3 minutes.
  • For group performances, the timing should not exceed 5 minutes.
  • Participants may choose their own talent relevant to a college audience.
  • Any vulgar or offensive content is strictly prohibited.
  • Participants are responsible for providing their own instruments.
  • All participants will receive a participation certificate.
  • Winners will receive exciting prizes.
  • If 5 or fewer entries are received, the event will be cancelled. Participants can choose either to receive a full refund or transfer to another event of the same price.
  • In case of a clash with any other committee event, no refund will be applicable.

Judging Criteria

  • Clarity and fluency in showcasing the talent.
  • Stage presence: confidence, engagement with the audience, and overall performance charisma.
  • Innovation and creativity.
