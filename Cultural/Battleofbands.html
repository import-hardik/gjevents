Rules and Regulations:

  • Bands interested in participating must complete registration before the specified deadline.
  • Each competing band should consist of a minimum of 4 members and may not exceed 6 members.
  • If the number of participating teams is less than 4, only the first prize will be awarded. If teams are more than 4, both first and second prizes will be awarded.
  • Bands are required to perform original compositions. While covers are permissible, original works are encouraged and will be given preference during judging.
  • Each band's performance slot will not exceed 18 minutes.
  • Bands are responsible for bringing their own instruments and necessary equipment.
  • Set-up and sound check time will be allocated to each band prior to their performance.
  • Prizes will be awarded to winners.
  • All participating bands will receive certificates acknowledging their participation.
  • In case of an event clash with any other committee event, no refund will be applicable.

Criteria for Evaluation:

  • Judges will assess bands based on their musicality, including instrumental proficiency, vocal quality, harmony, and overall cohesion.
  • Originality and creativity in composition and performance will be considered.
  • Stage presence, audience engagement, and overall charisma will also be evaluated.
  • Audience response and interaction during performances will factor into judging.
