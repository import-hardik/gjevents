Rules and Regulations

  • Groups must complete their registration before the specified deadline.
  • Groups must consist of a minimum of 4 and up to 6 members.
  • Groups have the liberty to choose their own music for the performance.
  • Selected music should be appropriate for the college audience and free from explicit or offensive content.
  • Choreography must be original or appropriately credited if adapted.
  • The costume should be according to the college‚Äôs code of conduct and must cover the body properly.

Performance Guidelines

  • Each group will have a maximum performance duration of 5 minutes.
  • The performance area will be clearly marked, and groups must confine their performance within this space.
  • Various dance styles, including contemporary, hip-hop, jazz, ballet, and cultural dances, are permitted.
  • Coordination, synchronization, and teamwork are essential elements of the performance.
  • If there are 5 or fewer entries, the event will be cancelled. In that case, participants can choose to receive a full refund or transfer to another event of the same price.
  • In case of event clash with any other committee event, no refund will be applicable.

Judging Criteria

  • Technique: Precision, synchronization, and execution of dance moves.
  • Expression: Emotion, storytelling, and audience engagement.
  • Stage Presence: Confidence, charisma, and overall performance quality.
  • Creativity: Originality of choreography, use of space, and thematic interpretation.
