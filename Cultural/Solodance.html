Rules and Regulations: Solo Dance

  • Participants may choose their theme-related song for their dance routine.
  • Music must be appropriate for a college audience and must not contain explicit or offensive language.
  • Costumes must be appropriate for the performance and must allow freedom of movement.
  • Participants are responsible for providing their own music in the required format (e.g., USB).
  • Each participant will be allocated a maximum of 3 minutes for their performance.
  • The performance area will be clearly defined, and participants must remain within the designated space.
  • Choreography must be original or properly credited if adapted from existing sources.
  • Winners will receive a special prize.
  • All participants will receive certificates of participation.
  • The venue will provide basic sound equipment including speakers and a music player.
  • If there are 5 or fewer entries, the event will be cancelled. Participants can choose to receive a full refund or transfer to another event of the same price.
  • In case of event clash with any other committee event, no refund will be applicable.

Judging Criteria:

  • Technique: Execution of dance moves, flexibility, control, and precision.
  • Artistry: Expression, emotion, interpretation of music, and storytelling ability.
  • Stage Presence: Confidence, charisma, engagement with the audience, and overall performance quality.
  • Originality: Uniqueness of choreography, creativity, and innovation.
