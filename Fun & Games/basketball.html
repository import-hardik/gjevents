Rules and Regulations

Girls - Singles Rules

  • 1 vs 1 format.
  • Match is best of 3 games, each game to 21 points.
  • Rally point system (point awarded on every rally).
  • Must win by 2 points; first to 30 wins if tied at 29-29.
  • Service:
    • Serve diagonally.
    • From right side at even score, left at odd score.
    • Serve must be underhand and below waist.
  • Court: Use narrow (inner) side lines, full length (long & narrow).

Boys - Doubles Rules

  • 2 vs 2 format.
  • Match is best of 3 games, each game to 21 points.
  • Rally point system (point awarded on every rally).
  • Serve Rotation:
    • Start from right service box (even score).
    • Partners switch sides after winning a point on serve.
    • If serving team loses a point, serve passes to opponents.
  • Service: Must land in the short & wide service box.
  • Court: Use full width, but shorter for serve.
