Gully Cricket - Rules and Regulations

  • It is a knockout event.
  • The losing team will be out of the event.
  • Gully cricket will be played in the tennis court area.
  • Jerk balling is not allowed.
  • Each team will be given 4 overs.
  • Only one player is allowed to bowl 2 overs out of 4. At least 3 bowlers must be present.
  • Total number of players permissible are 10. Only 7 players are allowed on the field at a time.
  • There is a rule of an impact player. 1 player can substitute according to the need of the team.
  • Any student without their college ID will not be allowed to play the respective game.
  • Any arguments with the match officials will lead to team disqualification.
