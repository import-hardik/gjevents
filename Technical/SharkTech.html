About the Event

HCST Shark Tank is a high-energy platform for aspiring student entrepreneurs to pitch their innovative start-up ideas to a distinguished panel of judges, including industry experts, startup mentors, and potential investors.

This one-day event offers participants the opportunity to showcase creativity, demonstrate market viability, and receive valuable feedback from real-world professionals. It’s more than just a competition — it’s a launchpad for the next big idea.

🔥 Highlights:

  • Present to top-tier judges and mentors.
  • Compete for exciting prizes and recognition.
  • Get real-world insights to refine your ideas.
  • Network with peers and industry professionals.

📜 Guidelines & Rules

👥 Team Composition:

  • Each team must consist of 2 to 5 members.
  • Interdisciplinary teams are encouraged.

💡 Idea Submission:

Start-up ideas can span across any industry, including but not limited to:

  • Technology
  • Healthcare
  • Education
  • Sustainability
  • Fintech
  • Social Innovation

All ideas must be:

  • Original (no plagiarism or previously funded ventures).
  • Innovative, feasible, and have clear market potential.

📝 Pre-Event Requirement:

  • Submit a concise business idea summary (max 200 words) for screening.
  • Submission should be emailed/shared by the deadline provided by the organizing team.
  • Teams are encouraged to include charts, visuals, or mock prototypes in their final presentation.

🖥️ Presentation Format:

  • Total Duration: 20 minutes per team
  • 15 minutes – Idea Pitch (PPT-based)
  • 5 minutes – Q&A with judges

Presentation must cover the following key areas:

  • Problem Statement
  • Business Solution / Idea
  • Revenue Model
  • Market Analysis
  • Financial Projections
  • Growth and Expansion Plan

📂 Submission Requirements:

  • All supporting materials (PowerPoint/PDF slides) must be submitted to organizers at least 15 minutes before the team’s scheduled pitch.
  • Only .pptx or .pdf formats will be accepted.
