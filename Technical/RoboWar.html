Competition Rulebook

1. Event Overview

Robot Wars is a combat robotics competition where participants build remote-controlled or autonomous robots to battle in an arena. The aim is to destroy, disable, or immobilize the opponentâ€™s robot using engineering creativity and strategic control.

2. Match Format

  • Match Style: 1v1 Knockout or Round-Robin (depends on the number of teams)
  • Match Duration: 3 minutes maximum
  • Victory Conditions:
    • Opponent is immobilized for 10 seconds
    • Opponent is pushed out of the arena (if using sumo-style ring)
    • Referee decision (points-based)

3. Robot Specifications

3.1 Dimensions

  • Maximum Size: 1 meter Ã— 1 meter Ã— 1 meter (adjustable based on arena)
  • Weight Limit: Less than 3.5 kg

3.2 Control

  • Allowed: Wired, Wireless (RC, Bluetooth, Wi-Fi)
  • Autonomous Robots: Allowed but must have safety override switch
  • Fail-safe: All bots must have a kill switch for emergency shutdown

3.3 Power Source

  • Maximum Power: 36V DC (No AC power allowed)
  • Internal Battery Packs: Must be safely enclosed

3.4 Weapon Systems

  • Allowed:
    • Spinners
    • Hammers
    • Wedges
    • Flippers
    • Lifting arms
  • Not Allowed:
    • Projectiles
    • Liquids (oil, water, etc.)
    • Fire/flame-based weapons
    • Explosives
    • EMP or signal jammers

3.5 Mobility

  • Allowed: Wheels, legs, tracks
  • Hover/Flying Bots: Not allowed unless specifically permitted

4. Arena Guidelines

  • Standard Arena Size: 10 ft Ã— 10 ft or larger
  • Safety Barriers: Will be provided
  • Clear Zone: For judges & audience

5. Safety Rules

  • All robots must pass a tech inspection before matches
  • Participants must wear safety gear during setup
  • No audience or team member allowed in the arena during the match
  • Unsafe bots will be disqualified immediately
  • Weapons must remain disabled outside the arena

6. Scoring (for Judged Matches)

  • Aggression: 30%
  • Control: 25%
  • Damage: 30%
  • Strategy: 15%

7. Team Guidelines

  • Each team can consist of up to 4 members
  • Teams must report to match 5 minutes in advance
  • All team members must sign a liability waiver

8. Disqualification Rules

  • Unsportsmanlike behavior
  • Intentional arena damage
  • Late arrival (5 min grace period)
  • Use of banned components

9. Prizes & Recognition

  • Winner, Runner-up, and Best Design awards
  • Certificates for all participants
  • Special award for Most Creative Weapon or Best Defense

10. Miscellaneous

  • All decisions by the judges and organizers are final
  • Organizers reserve the right to modify rules on the fly to ensure safety and fairness
  • NOTE: Organizers are not liable for damage to bots during the fight
