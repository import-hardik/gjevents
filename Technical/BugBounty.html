Bug Bounty - Rulebook



Round 1: Code Debugging

  • Participants will be given 10 code snippets containing bugs.
  • Time Limit: 30 minutes.
  • Task: Identify the errors in each code and mention the type of error (syntax error, logical error, runtime error, etc.).
  • Partial answers (only stating the error without type) may receive partial marks.
  • Top performers will qualify for Round 2 based on accuracy and completeness.

Round 2: Algorithm Debugging

  • Qualified participants from Round 1 will proceed to Round 2.
  • Task: Debug a given algorithm and ensure it works correctly.
  • Time Limit: 30 minutes.
  • Participants must explain the changes they made if asked by the organizers.

Tie Breaker (If Necessary)

  • If there is a tie after Round 2, an additional round will be conducted.
  • Participants will be given a short code or logic problem to debug within a limited time (e.g., 10 minutes).
  • First to solve correctly wins.

General Instructions

  • Use of the internet or external help is strictly prohibited unless explicitly allowed.
  • Plagiarism or copying from others will lead to disqualification.
  • Participants must submit their answers within the time limit. Late submissions will not be accepted.
  • Organizersâ€™ decisions regarding results and disputes will be final and binding.
  • Respect fellow participants and maintain a competitive but healthy environment.
