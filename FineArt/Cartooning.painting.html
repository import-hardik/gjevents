Rules and Regulations

  • Chart paper will be provided by the Organizers.
  • Time allotted: 30 Minutes.
  • The participants will carry their own water bottle, stationary material, and rough cloth or tissue as per your need.
  • No phones are allowed during the competition.
  • Evaluation will be based on the theme, quality, difficulty of work, its artistic value, and overall presentation. The final score will be the average of the Judges' evaluation.
  • Participation certificates will be given to all the participants.
